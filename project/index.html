About Us?

Passion to be better than yesterday and grow each day drives us. Quest to move up the ladder at every stage of functioning has led to set-up of Synovest Capital. Here we create synergies by providing innovative solutions to potential and existing entrepreneurs enabling scale-up of their businesses on one hand, and on the other we identify and offer unique opportunities to investors to assume relevant exposure and be a part of a successful entrepreneurial journey.

Besides being duly qualified and experienced in diverse domains viz. general management, strategy, finance, operations and technical we, at Synovest, are a team of first generation entrepreneurs and investors ourselves and thus, very well construe the requirements and gaps on both sides and endeavour to bridge the same.

Services

Start-up consulting



We are a true set-up partners for starts ups. We provide 360 degree commercial mentorship and hand-holding to our start-ups partners so that their concepts see light of the day in real world.

Right from vision build-up and optimal conceptualization to build-up of business plans and business valuations to getting the first client and right investors, we assist new age entrepreneurs and actively participate at every stage of commercialization.

Growth/Scale-up Consulting



Where you are seeking a professional partner to scale up your venture, we shall assist you in creating a road map to achieve your vision and even participate in your scale-up journey if the need be e.g. by consummating successful commercial tie-ups, assembly of right resources and financial assistance through individual and financial institutions.

Investor Platform and Investment Management



We facilitate unique and diverse investment opportunities to our investors on-board. These opportunities are thoroughly screened as per their investment strategy and objective.

We act as Investment Managers, if the need be, where we represent our investors on investee board, as nominees, to supervise investee functioning and provide strategic, operational and financial guidance to achieve the desired business growth, returns and overall investment objective.

Investment



Synovest does not restrict itself to only advisory services but also takes active investment exposure, especially in start-ups. Synovest itself or the team in individual capacities may invest in opportunities as per their investment objective.

Business & Finance Restructuring



Backed up successful experience of restructuring and turnaround of international companies and diverse and highly experienced core management team, we provide varied restructuring services with an objective to increase shareholder's value. These services include but not limited to:

  • Management restructuring
  • Operations restructuring
  • Marketing & product placement course correction
  • Profit & Loss Account restructuring
  • Balance Sheet restructuring (incl. optimal Capital Structuring and Working Capital Management)
